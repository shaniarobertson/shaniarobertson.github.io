What is Modding?

"Modding" is a term that is short for moderating. Twitch's definition is: "Moderators ensure that the chat meets the behavior and content standards set by the broadcaster by removing offensive posts and spam that detracts from conversations". This includes ensuring content abides by both Twitch's and Discord's Terms of Service.
